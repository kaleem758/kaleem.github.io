About Me

I am a dedicated nanophotonics researcher currently focusing on cutting-edge technologies in integrated photonics, 2D materials, and metasurfaces. My work spans the design and development of advanced photonic devices, with a particular interest in leaky-wave metasurfaces and 2D materials integrated photonics, contributing to a new understanding of light-matter interactions. Over the course of my academic and professional journey, I have developed expertise in the fabrication, characterization, and theoretical analysis of 2D materials and nanophotonics systems. My previous research includes investigating harmonic generation in low-dimensional materials and exploring advanced super-resolution imaging techniques. I have worked across prestigious institutions, including the University of Delaware and Nanjing University, contributing to projects at the frontier of optical engineering and nanoscience.
